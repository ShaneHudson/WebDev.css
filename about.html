Information About The Linington Nature Reserve

The Linington Nature Reserve is cultivated in order to achieve a diverse environment full of separated habitats containing a large multitude of wildlife. Linington Nature Reserve has a field, a large and a small pond, and some woodland. There are several varieties of fish as well as yellow irises and reeds in both of our ponds. You will also find many birds flying around the reserve such as avocet, black swan, sedge warblers and woodpeckers in the woodland. Our field is home to many rabbits, red squirrels and water buffalo.
