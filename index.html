Welcome to Linington Nature Reserve

The Linington Nature Reserve is an area of land on the edge of the village of Stourville, cultivated so as to encourage a diversity of wildlife.

Opening times

10am - 3pm on Saturdays and Sundays within the months of April to October.

£2 per visit

How To Find Us

We are situated on the edge of Stourville near Reading, which is just off the M4 at junction 4
